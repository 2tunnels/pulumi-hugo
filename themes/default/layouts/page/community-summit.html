Frequently Asked Questions

Who should attend?

Any practitioner, engineer, developer or professional who is interested in Pulumi and Cloud Engineering. Feel free to invite your friends and colleagues. Cloud Engineering practitioners include infrastructure (DevOps, SREs), platform engineers, application developers, and security professionals who are building modern cloud-based applications and infrastructure. They help their organizations achieve digital transformation goals and address both the challenges and opportunities posed by rapid innovation, scale, reliability, security, and compliance.

What are appropriate topics?

Suggested topics could include K8's, Azure, Migrations, Python, specifics about using Pulumi, culture or gaming are fun to discuss. Even pets are fair game - feel free to bring yours along for the summit.

Do I have to travel?

As travel restrictions and comfort levels change, we may introduce this event in-person but for now, we will host a virtual environment for our community to gather, discuss and network.
